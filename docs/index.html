Bookmote compatibility list

Bookmote turns pages on your e-reader or tablet over Bluetooth HID, straight from your phone's volume keys or on-screen buttons — even with your phone's screen off. Here's what's confirmed to work.

Get Bookmote on Google Play

Confirmed

  • Apple Books
  • Kindle for iOS
  • Google Play Books
  • Adobe Acrobat (PDF)
  • RIDI
  • Kyobo eBook
  • Millie
  • yes24
  • Kindle for Android
  • Google Play Books (Android)
  • Adobe Acrobat (PDF, Android)
  • KOReader Verified
  • Onyx BOOX series Verified
  • Onyx BOOX Go 7
  • Kobo Clara 2E
  • Kobo Clara BW
  • Kobo Clara Colour
  • Kobo Libra 2
  • Kobo Libra Colour
  • Kobo Sage
  • Kobo Elipsa 2E
  • Crema (yes24 e-reader) Verified
  • Other Android-based e-readers
  • Supernote (Ratta)
  • iPad and iPhone
  • Android tablets and phones

Exceptions

  • reMarkable (Paper Pro, Move, etc.)
  • Jailbroken Kindle + KOReader Verified

Not compatible

  • Kindle devices (Paperwhite, Oasis, Scribe)
  • Older Kobo without Bluetooth

Found something wrong or out of date? Report it on GitHub — corrections and new reports are always welcome.
