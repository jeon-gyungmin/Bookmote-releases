Does Bookmote work with Onyx BOOX series?

Yes — Bookmote works with Onyx BOOX series.

Verified

Page turn, home, and e-ink refresh all work

How to pair

  1. On your e-reader or tablet, open Bluetooth settings and put it in pairing mode.
  2. On your phone, open Bookmote and choose "Pair a device" to connect over Bluetooth HID.
  3. Once connected, turn pages with your phone's volume keys or Bookmote's on-screen buttons — even with your phone's screen off.

Known limitations

Most e-readers put their Bluetooth radio to sleep after a period of inactivity to save battery. If page turns stop responding, tap your e-reader's screen to wake it — Bookmote reconnects automatically once the device is awake.

Works with these reading apps

Bookmote has been confirmed to work with: Apple Books (iOS app), Kindle for iOS (iOS app), Google Play Books (iOS app), Adobe Acrobat (PDF) (iOS app), RIDI (iOS app), Kyobo eBook (iOS app), Millie (iOS app), yes24 (iOS app), Kindle for Android (Android app), Google Play Books (Android) (Android app), Adobe Acrobat (PDF, Android) (Android app), KOReader (Android app).
